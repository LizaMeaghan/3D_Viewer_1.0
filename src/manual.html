Program features

This is the simple 3D viewer, which opens obj files and reads only vertices and faces

Functions

  • Open obj files
  • Rotate figures along three axes
  • Move shapes along three axes
  • Take a screenshots
  • Choose display options such as background color, polygon color, line color, etc.

Control

The control panel is closed by default. To open it, you need to click on the upper left corner - Sidebar and click on the show / hide button. This will be possible only after opening a file. To open a file, click on the top right - File - Open and select the desired file in the window that opens. To close, click - File - Close. To take a screenshot, click - Record - Screenshot.

Control Panel

After opening the control panel, you will have 3 sliders in front of you, they are needed to rotate the figure in the corresponding axis. The buttons under each slider are responsible for moving the shape in space. There are also buttons that increase and decrease the size of the figure, which is sometimes useful.
